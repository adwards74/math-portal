            <h3>1. The Pythagorean Trinity</h3>
            <p>The identity $\sin^2\theta + \cos^2\theta = 1$ is just the Pythagorean theorem applied to the unit circle. From this, we derive the identities for $\sec, \tan, \csc, \text{ and } \cot$.</p>
